EXPLORING WEATHER TRENDS¶

In this project, local and global temperature data are analysed and the temperature trends are compared between the local temperature trends with overall global temperature trends.

Observations¶

Below are some observations that has been made based on the resultant weather trend over the last few decades.

  1. As clearly depicted, Delhi is much hotter compared to the global average temperature. The difference of temperature (around 15 C) has been consistent over time.
  2. Changes in Delhi's temperature has been overall consistent with the global temperature trend over time but there is sudden rise in Delhi's temperature around 1900.
  3. Looking at the trend, it is observed that the world is getting hotter slowly. Also there is a slight more increase in the temperature level specially after 1975.
  4. A sudden drop in temperature is also observed between 1800 and 1825.
